7. Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently gathered such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

If you are located in a jurisdiction with data protection laws (such as the GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. You may also opt out of any future communication from us at any time by contacting us directly.
