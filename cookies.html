This Cookies Policy explains how My Recipe Book ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using this site, you agree to our use of cookies as described below. If you do not agree, you can modify your browser settings or stop using the website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze user behavior to enhance performance and content delivery.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (which are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which stay on your device for a longer period or until you delete them).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic operation of the site. For example, they help you navigate pages and access secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with the site by collecting anonymous usage data. This helps us improve design and functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: These allow the site to remember choices you’ve made (such as your name or preferences) to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use third-party analytics tools like Google Analytics to track user behavior and trends. These cookies collect aggregated data for statistical purposes.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we may use third-party services that place their own cookies on your device. These may include analytics providers, embedded video services (e.g., YouTube), or social media sharing tools. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective providers’ policies and not by us.

4. Your Choices and Cookie Control

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline them if you prefer. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and performance of the site.

You can delete cookies at any time by clearing your browser history. Additionally, some browsers allow you to install extensions or plugins that offer more advanced privacy controls.

If you wish to opt out of tracking by Google Analytics, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
